<span id="page-127-4"></span>Hardware icons represent devices as you most often see them: on your desk. Because these devices are also frequently handled and carried, people are familiar with them as three-dimensional objects with weight. The Aqua treatment of hardware icons reinforces their association with real objects.

![](images/_page_127_Picture_14.jpeg)  
To help users distinguish between external devices, their icons provide a region for an identifying symbol (FireWire, SCSI, and so on).
