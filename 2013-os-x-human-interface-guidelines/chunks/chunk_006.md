In addition to the chapters listed above, *Mac OS X Human Interface Guidelines* contains two appendixes:  
- ["Keyboard](#page-307-0) Shortcuts" (page 308) provides some guidance on creating custom keyboard shortcuts in your app and lists the system-reserved and commonly used keyboard shortcuts in OS X.
- ["System-Provided](#page-318-0) Icons" (page 319) lists the icons and images that OS X provides and describes how you should use them in your app.
