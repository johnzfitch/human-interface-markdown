#### ■ **Height:**  
❏ Full size: 20 pixels  
❏ Small: 17 pixels  
❏ Mini: 15 pixels  
■ **Width:** Wide enough to accommodate the longest menu item  
#### ■ **Spacing:**  
❏ Full size: Leave at least 12 pixels of space between stacked controls.  
❏ Small: At least 10 pixels  
❏ Mini: At least 8 pixels
